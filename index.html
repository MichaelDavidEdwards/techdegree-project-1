Background

Greetings & salutations! My name is Michael Edwards. I'm a graphic artist and photographer based in northeastern Pennsylvania. Between my education and in the workplace, I have over a decade of experience in the field - three years experience of traditional printing and running jobs on a single colour offset printing press, seven years (combined) working for two publishing company as a graphic artist designing advertisements for retail and automotive companies and four years of working in a digital print department as a print consultant. I have a degree in computer graphics specialization and photography. Throughout the years, I have used various industry standard page layout, image editing, web design, illustration, animation and newspaper publishing applications.

I’m an aspiring web designer who loves everything about the web. I've have worked in lots of different jobs. I’m excited to bring my life experience to the process of building fantastic looking websites.

I’m a life-long learner who's always interested in expanding my skills.
